Our Story

Founded in 2020, My Airbnb began with a simple mission: to provide travelers with authentic, comfortable, and memorable stays across West Africa. What started as a small collection of handpicked properties has grown into a trusted platform connecting travelers with exceptional accommodations.

Our Mission

We're dedicated to creating meaningful connections between travelers and local communities, offering unique stays that reflect the rich culture and hospitality of West Africa. Our goal is to make every stay an opportunity for cultural exchange and unforgettable memories.

Hospitality

We believe in creating warm, welcoming environments where every guest feels at home.

Quality

Every property is carefully selected and maintained to ensure the highest standards of comfort.

Culture

We celebrate and share the rich cultural heritage of our locations with our guests.
